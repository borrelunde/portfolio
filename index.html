About Me

I'm a passionate programmer and technology enthusiast from Bergen, Norway. With a background in computer electronics and a deep interest in software development, I've built skills across various programming languages and technologies.

I believe in continuous learning and challenging myself with new projects that push my boundaries. My technical expertise spans programming, mechanical design, and electronics — allowing me to approach problems from multiple perspectives and create combined solutions.

When I'm not coding, I explore Norwegian landscapes with my camera, read up on history, study languages, or enjoy a game of chess.
